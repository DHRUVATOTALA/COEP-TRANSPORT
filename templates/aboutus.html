Our Mission:


Our mission is simple yet powerful: to simplify the lives of COEP students by providing them with easy access
to accurate schedules, timely updates, and efficient public transportation options. We understand the challenges students
face in managing their time and navigating the complexities of campus life, and we are dedicated to making those challenges a thing of the past.


What we offer:


  • Up-to-Date Schedules:

    Never miss a class, event, or deadline again. Our website provides real-time updates on class schedules, exams, extracurricular activities, and more, ensuring that students are always informed and prepared.

  • Public Transport Integration:

    Getting around campus and the city just got easier. Our portal offers comprehensive information on public transportation options, including routes, timetables, and nearby stops, empowering students to travel conveniently and efficiently.
